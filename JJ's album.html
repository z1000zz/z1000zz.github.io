Drifter / Like You Do (2020)

Drifter / Like You Do is the fourteenth studio album and first double album by Singaporean singer JJ Lin, released on 20 October 2020, through Warner Music. The first disc, Drifter, contains six Mandarin songs including the single "No Turning Back", which reached number one on the China TME Uni Chart. The second disc, Like You Do, contains eight English songs including the single "Bedroom" featuring Anne-Marie, which was released on 22 April 2021.
