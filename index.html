JJ Lin

JJ Lin is a Chinese singer-songwriter and producer from Singapore. He has been in the music industry for more than 20 years and has created legends in the Chinese music scene with hits such as "Jiangnan" and "Practice Love". He has won four Golden Melody Awards. This page collects his latest singles, tour status, complete album chronology, etc., so you can have a glimpse of JJ's musical universe and warm energy at one go.
